About Me

So far throughout my life I have worked in many different fields, From coaching gymnastics to working on a construction crew. While each of these endeavors has helped me develop certain skills such as time management, problem solving, how to act in high pressure situations, and multi-tasking, all of them lacked something that could keep my mind active. That is what lead me to software development, the idea of continuously learning and creating new things My focus is in C# and .NET framework. I have always been around the development world, my Mom and Dad have worked in the field my whole life and recently my brother has started his career in software development as well. At the end of the day I like to be able to see the work I have put in come to life and become something I am proud of.
